The Rules
of Battleship

How To Win

Sink all of your opponents ships before they sink yours!

How To Play

Place all of your ships on the player board. When you're ready, start the game. Take turns guessing where you think the enemy has hidden their ships. Guess all of their ships first and you win the game!
